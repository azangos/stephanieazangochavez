AMSTERDAM

Amsterdam is such a beautiful place to visit. From the rich history to the risque attractions, there is something for everyone.

A list of places to visit are:

  • Anne Frank's House
  • The Canals
  • The Bulldog
  • Moulin Rouge

The best time to visit are as follows:

  1. Summer
  2. Spring
  3. Autumn
  4. Winter
